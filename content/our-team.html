The Helen Charles Difference

Our mission is to provide the highest quality and the most cost effective service in the hair and beauty industry, to satisfy our clients each and every time they visit the salon in weston, fl and retain their custom. We believe in continuing education. We provide all team members with ongoing training as well as support from manufacturers and leading industry educators. In our continued effort to provide our clients with the best qualified service providers, we operate a "Roam Free Policy", where clients can feel free to try any service provider they wish.

HELEN CHARLES | Salon Owner

Master Stylist & Color Specialist Originally from London, Helen was trained at Vidal Sassoon and owned a chain of 4 salons in North London. From phenomenal up-dos to precision coloring, Helen's dynamic techniques have drawn worldwide attention. She has consistently been linked with industry leaders, most notable Goldwell, Wella, L'Oreal and Vidal Sassoon and enjoys a reputation as a vibrant and charismatic innovator. Today she exudes and expands her genius on both sides of the Atlantic, teaching others as well as her team in Florida the intricacies of hair that she has gained over the years. Helen leads her salon's creative team and continuously encourages their creativity to be pushed to great heights through stage events and photographic shoot
