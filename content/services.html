Keratin Treatment

Get straight, get smooth, get sexy hair with our Keratin Treatment. This new revolutionary smoothing system infuses Keratin deep into the hair cuticle eliminating up to 95% of frizz and curl from the hair, leaving the hair soft, shiny and luxurious. The more you treat your hair the healthier it becomes with each treatment, the buildup of Keratin makes the hair softer & silkier, straighter & shinier, faster & easier to blow dry and reduces daily styling time. The treatment needs to be repeated 3 to 6 months for maximum benefits.

Keratin Spa

This treatment is beneficial to correct issues due to chemical services. The Keratin Spa can also be used in between Keratin Treatments to extend the life of the Keratin results in your hair. Similar to the Keratin Treatment, the Keratin Spa is a lighter version of the same product, hair can be washed and styled as usual.
